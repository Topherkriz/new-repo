Which Generation Subaru STI I favor in ranking order

2000 STI

Ranking in number 4 who is last but not least, it is the 2000 STI Impreza. At this point a collectors item, with a price ticket that can buy a small home. The 2000 STI Impreza is the ONLY STI that is in the coupe version. Of course the smaller chassis with almost the same power as the rest, this Impreza kicked started the industry to its course today.

2002 STI

Coming in at number 1 it is the 2002 Bugeye STI. A thing to note about this STI is that just like its older STI sibling this was only offered in japan. It's weird funky headlights with giant fog lights gave it it's name for that. I always enjoyed this chassis because it was the starting point for the "Legoparts that became interchangable. Although you can drop a EJ207 on all of these chassis' this generation is the one I would love to have with that engine.

2004 STI

Spot number 2 goes to yours truly the 2004 Blobeye STI. The thing about this car is that it was the car that brought most of not ALL JDM kids to want this car. All the car games had at least this version of the STI, I know I did I would always go for it. Even though I could not afford it when it forst came out new I was still an early teenager. Of course theres always am opportunity to get a something like this, yes, thats right I was able to get my hands on this generation STI. Even though I rated it second, I choose it because it came with all the STI goodies that I did want to spend later on more money. This was the next best thing.

2007 STI

Running in 3rd the next Impreza is the Hawkeye STI this generation was said to be the "bulletproof USDM version of the EJ family. Compared to it;s older siblings, the 2007 STI has the best gearing ratios and best interior options at the time. Many people take the front end of the Hawkeye and end up having their older gen STI look more newer. Not my most favorite but not the least favorite.
